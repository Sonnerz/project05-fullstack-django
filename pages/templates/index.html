CAM-PAL

CAM-PAL is smart software that suggests settings based on your subject and environment. It picks the optimal settings for any scene and then it lets you control the final shot.

CAM-PAL customises settings based on the lens and camera you're using, along with how they perform in different situations. CAM-PAL will move settings to avoid your cameras weaknesses. It can then choose the perfect shutter speed to get sharp photos without noise.

CAM-PAL

This is a support site for CAM-PAL. This is where CAM-PAL users can come and submit any issues they have with the software or request new features to be developed.

Bugs can be added without charge. We will endeavour to address any issues that users submit for review.

Requests for new features to be investigated will require a minimum purchase of 2 hours at €50 per hour. Each feature will require a minimum of 10 hours (€500) to be raised before the CAM-PAL developers will consider developing a requested feature.

If you request a new feature or donate towards the development of a new feature you will gain access to our Blog area where you can share your Photography stories and experiences with us.
